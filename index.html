Key Features

Automated Driver Scheduling

Dynamically assigns drivers for high-value pick-ups and coordinates inter-center item transfers based on inventory needs.

Real-Time Donation Tracking

Allows users to monitor the status of donations in real time, creating a transparent experience.

Inventory Management Dashboard

Enables admins to track donations, verify incoming items, and manage distribution from a centralized interface.

Route Optimization for Drivers

Provides real-time, shortest-path navigation to donor or volunteer addresses.

Data-Driven Decision Making

Empowers organizations to manage their inventory proactively with real-time data on inventory levels and donation trends
